Children's Day Celebration!

On the occasion of Children's Day,Magnificient Therapy Services invites you to celebrate the remarkable journey of children with special needs. Join us in honoring their resilience, joy, and boundless potential on this special day dedicated to their well-being.

At Magnificient Therapy Services, we understand the unique challenges faced by children with special needs, and we are committed to providing them with comprehensive therapy and support. On Children's Day, we aim to raise awareness about their abilities, while advocating for inclusivity and equal opportunities for every child.

Our Children's Day celebration will be a day filled with fun, learning, and togetherness. We have planned exciting activities, interactive sessions, and engaging workshops tailored to the specific needs and interests of each child. Through these activities, we strive to foster their growth, boost their confidence, and create lasting memories.

Moreover, this celebration will provide an opportunity for parents, caregivers, and our dedicated team of therapists to come together and share experiences, knowledge, and resources. It will be a day of collaboration and empowerment, where we reaffirm our commitment to providing the best possible care for children with special needs.

We invite you to be a part of this meaningful celebration by joining us on Children's Day. The event shall commenced by 10:00am at Magnificient Therapy Compound, refreshment will be made available for Parents and Caregivers Let's come together to celebrate the resilience and potential of children with special needs on this joyous occasion of Children's Day.

Walk for Cerebral Palsy

Get ready to step up and make a difference...

#WalkForCerebralPalsy!

Get ready to step up and make a difference at our Annual Walk for Cerebral Palsy! Lace up your walking shoes and join us as we come together to raise awareness and support for children with cerebral palsy.

At Magnificient Therapy Services, we are committed to improving the lives of children with cerebral palsy through specialized therapy and compassionate care. Our Annual Walk for Cerebral Palsy is a powerful event that brings the community together, creating a platform for advocacy, unity, and hope.

This walk is not just a physical journey; it symbolizes the strides we are taking towards a more inclusive society. By participating, you become an advocate for children with cerebral palsy, spreading awareness about their unique challenges and capabilities. Every step you take and every naira you raise will directly contribute to providing essential therapy services and resources to these children and their families.

Our Annual Walk for Cerebral Palsy is an opportunity for individuals, families, businesses, and community organizations to show their support. Whether you walk as a team or as an individual, your participation will make a meaningful impact on the lives of these remarkable children.

All participant are to convene at the Yaba LCDA by 8:00am on Thursday the 1st of April 2024, as there shall be our starting point as we walk from Yaba LCDA to Havey Road to LASCOETH to Moloney to Unilag junction to Alagomeji to Sabo to Atan to Radiography to Medical College to Simpson and back to Yaba LCDA, where all our guests would be enlighten and addressed

Join us as we take steps towards a brighter future for children with cerebral palsy. Together, let's make a difference and empower these extraordinary individuals to reach their full potential.

Christmas Carol

Celebrate the magic of the holiday season with us!

Christmas Carol!

Celebrate the magic of the holiday season with Magnificient Therapy Services at our upcoming Christmas Carol Celebration! Join us as we gather to spread joy, warmth, and cheer among children with special needs and their families.

At Magnificient Therapy Services, we believe that every child deserves a festive and inclusive celebration. Our Christmas Carol Celebration is a heartwarming event designed to create unforgettable memories for the children we serve. Through the power of music, singing, and togetherness, we aim to create an enchanting experience that celebrates the spirit of the season.

During the event, children will have the opportunity to participate in lively and interactive Christmas carol sessions tailored to their unique abilities. Our team of dedicated therapists will guide them through the songs, ensuring a joyful and inclusive experience for everyone involved. The celebration will also include fun-filled activities, such as arts and crafts, games, and special surprises for the children.

We warmly welcome parents, caregivers, volunteers, and supporters to join us in this celebration. It's a perfect opportunity to connect, share stories, and embrace the joy of the season in a welcoming and inclusive environment.

As we embark on this heartwarming journey, we invite you to contribute to the magic of the Christmas Carol Celebration. Whether it's through your presence, volunteering, or making a donation, your support will help us create an unforgettable experience for these incredible children and their families.

The Event Shall be taking place at The University of Lagos Main Auditorium by 2:00pm. All invitees are enjoined to be present as seat will not be kept for anyone. Wishing you all a joyous and harmonious holiday season!
