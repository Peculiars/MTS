CAREER

Be Part of Something Bigger

At Magnificient Therapy, no position is ever “just a job.” From nurses, direct caregivers, and therapists, to medical professionals, administrators and everything in between, a career at Magnificient Therapy allows you to leave work everyday knowing that you have helped to improve the lives of children and families.

As a non-profit organization, Magnificient Therapy lives at the crossroad of compassion for children and expert healthcare. We offer specialty services, uniquely equipped facilities, and state-of-the-art technology to children with special healthcare needs and their families. Magnificient Therapy’s staff and community members love working together to help kids and young adults live better lives. Our emphasis on caring for our staff member’s needs, professional development, and education can be seen through our contributions.

High staff engagement and satisfaction scores

Our mission is simple: We partner with families to help children with special healthcare needs live a great life. The children and young adults we serve inspire us to provide possibilities and improve quality of life in a caring environment.

Looking for an Internship?

Experience in the workforce makes a resume shine. Magnificient Therapy provides internship opportunities with the student in mind.

Our paid or for-credit internship opportunities take place in Magnificient Therapy’s dynamic, fast-paced environment. We provide realistic workplace experience across multiple departments. Upon the completion of an internship, there may be a possibility for full-time employment.

Internships in the following areas may be available:

Therapy

Human Resources

Learning and Development

Communication and Marketing

Social Work

Health Care

VOLUNTEER at Magnificient Therapy Services

Making a Difference

Magnificient Therapy volunteers make an enormous difference in the lives of the kids and young adults we serve. We have many volunteer opportunities like Social work, Medical Care, HKHE, preparing or helping with a craft, and supporting fun events throughout the year. We welcome energetic, enthusiastic individuals and groups who pass our background check process.

Jump In!

Magnificient Therapy has many volunteer opportunities to consider! We will match your skills and interests with one or more of the following opportunities as they are needed:

Great Life Champions: Help us bring our mission to life by playing games, prepping crafts, and helping with activities.

Special Skills & Talents:Put your musical gifts, dancing prowess, sewing skills, woodworking mastery, arts and crafts abilities to great use.

Special Event Support:Help us bring our fun-filled events to life through event prep, set-up, or clean-up; running a game or activity, helping with check-in, and more.

Administrative Support:Are you task-oriented and love to see a job well-done? Help various services through filing, data entry, assembling packets, making photocopies, preparing crafts, and more.

Professional Volunteer:From landscaping to fundraising, we love hearing from professionals looking to donate their expertise across many fields.
